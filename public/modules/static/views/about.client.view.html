Cultural losses due to globalization

SenseForage started out in 2014 as a project intended to preserve the unique cultures around the world from globalization. With emerging technologies, the distance between countries has drastically decreased, and this has led to losses of many important aspects of cultures around the world. Traditional industries are no longer able to compete in the their respective markets because everything has become price-driven, instead of quality-driven. Sacrifices were made in order to stay competitive, and many traditions have been lost in the process.
